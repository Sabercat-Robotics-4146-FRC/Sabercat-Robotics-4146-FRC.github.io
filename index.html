Student organized. Community supported.

FRC team 4146 is student organized and always has been. We define our own hierarchy and structure. We design, fabricate, and assemble an FRC robot each year. We build our own brand, and radiate excellence in everything we do.

Performance education. World class team.

Our team is composed of the most talented individuals. Both the students and the mentors are extremely dedicated to the team. We train our members in all aspects of running a nonprofit organization dedicated to designing world class robots.
